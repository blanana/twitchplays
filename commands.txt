right x - hold right for x seconds |
left x  - hold left for x seconds  | 1-25 seconds
up x    - hold up for x seconds    | (float vaues work too)
down x  - hold down for x seconds  |

stop - stop movement

jump       - press up for 0,1 seconds
jump right - press right and up for 0.2 seconds
jump left  - press left and up for 0.2 seconds

use     - press control for 1 second
hold    - hold control
release - release control
enter   - press enter for 0,1 seconds

set_limit <number> - changes time limit for right, left, up, down

--------------------------
hotkeys:
L - toggles all Commands
7 - toggles command Chance
both are off on startup
